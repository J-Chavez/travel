Cartagena de Indias

Visit Cartagena


Cartagena is a a beatiful town located in Colombia. It is in the caribbean coast and known for its amazing beaches and historic old town. The colonial architecture attracts millions of visitors annualy. It is a very safe town to visit and I find it to be more affordable than similar vacation destinations.


Plan a trip with your family and friends or you can go alone and make friends on your visit! You will have a great time sightseeing, visiting beaches, tasting local foods, and partying with the locals. There is something for everyone in Cartagena.
